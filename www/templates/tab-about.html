Classic Jewellery has a brand heritage spanning 32 years and prides itself on exquisite timeless designs, quality and the personal touch. Its Founder Mr Kelvin Lee was the region’s sought after trader in precious gemstones. Kelvin is the only Singaporean who has sold millions of dollars of precious gemstones in Asia, Europe, America and Australia to major factories, wholesalers, retailers and collectors in the jewellery industry including industry giants. He was the first Sales Manager of Gemimpex, a wholesale company for jewellery set up by 3 Jews who later set up Mondial. Kelvin was the Joint MD of the Precious Stones Exchange Pte Ltd and was featured in The Straits Times, Business Times and the Chinese local press. He was also the MD of Muzo Mines Columbia International Pte Ltd. In the industry, he has gained a reputation as the “King of Emeralds”, sourcing emeralds from Bogota in Columbia and selling them in international markets. His esteemed clientele include Larry Jewellery, B.P de Silva, Mohan Department Store, K.M Oli Mohd Jewellery, Jetaime Jewellery, Mondial Jewellery, Poh Heng Goldsmith & Jewellery, just to name a few, as well as the royalty in Malaysia. He has spent 6 years globe trotting retailing gemstones and learning the ropes of funding in America since 1986.

Having been blessed by a family business that gave him good fortune in the millions, Kelvin believes in giving back to society. In 1994, he became the wedding jewellery sponsor for Singapore TV stars Li Nanxing and Yang Libing, much to the delight of the couple.

Today he is the General Manager of Classic Jewellery (W) Pte Ltd. He pursues his passion of precious gemstones and jewellery by teaching and sharing his rich experience and knowledge, leaving his rich legacy of Classic to whomever has an interest to pursue a highly rewarding and deeply satisfying career as a jeweller.

Classic Jewellery’s business philosophy is “Making Quality and Precious Jewellery Affordable”. To Kelvin, jewellery is the Universal Language of Love. His passion drives him to create more and more simple yet stunning designs to adorn his clients locally and overseas.
